AT YOUR DISPOSAL

Discover our services designed to make the festive season even more special: looking for presents will be a real pleasure.

ONLINE SHIPPING AND RETURNS

Receive your products in a simple, quick and secure way wherever you prefer using our delivery options. Standard shipping is free for all registered users who place an order when logged in. Once your order is completed, we will keep you informed of all the shipping stages and you can track the status of your order here. Please note that you can return or exchange any product within 14 days of the delivery date: simply complete the online exchange and Return form and book a collection with our courier.

IN-STORE RETURNS

If you have made an online purchase and you are not convinced, you can conveniently return it to any store offering this service within 14 days of delivery. Our staff will active the returns procedure and manage the return of the products.

BOOK AN APPOINTMENT

Book your in-store appointment and enjoy an Armani shopping experience in complete safety: the perfect way to discover the collections, get the latest news about this season’s trends and get new outfit ideas. Choose your preferred store, date and time and book your appointment.

IN-STORE COLLECTION

If you prefer, you can pick up your online order free of charge at the most convenient store for you : add your product to the Shopping Bag,on the shopping bag page, select the “pick up in store” option and select your nearest store Once the order is completed, we will notify you of all the shipping stages and we will send you a collection confirmation email when your products are available in store.

ALWAYS THERE FOR YOU

We are always there to help you with the shopping process or provide you with the necessary support afterwards. You can choose what is the most convenient way for you to get answers to any doubts you may have.

CLICK & RESERVE

If you prefer to try on the item you want to buy you can use our "Click & Reserve" service: choose the item you are interested in, check its availability in your nearest store & click on “Book” to reserve it Once in store, you can decide whether or not to buy the item.

EASY, SECURE PAYMENT

Every payment takes place in accordance with the highest security standards. You can choose the most convenient method for you from several payment methods; we accept credit cards, PayPal, Klarna or cash on delivery.
