Recipe:

Baked Grapes and Whipped Feta Bruschetta

It's the perfect finger food for sharing, setting the stage for conversation and connection as you savor each delicious bite.

What do we need to know and where do we start?

Indulge in a delightful symphony of sweet and savory with this recipe. Let the caramelized grapes and creamy spread transport you and your loved one to a culinary escape.

Ingredients

You will need the following:

  • 2 cups grapes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 slices wholemeal bread (or ciabatta bread)
  • 2 tbsp feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest (grated)
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp basil (torn leaves)

Instructions

Let's begin with:

  1. Preheat oven to 180℃
  2. Place the grapes on a baking tray and drizzle over the olive oil and bake for 30 minutes till blistered and oozing juices, remove from oven
  3. Toast the bread and rub with the cut side of the garlic on top side
  4. Toast the pine nuts in a dry pan for about 1 minute till turning golden and aromatic, remove from pan immediately
  5. Break up the feta with a fork and whip in the Greek yogurt till smooth and stir through the grated lemon zest
  6. Spoon the feta mixture on to the toast and top with the baked grapes
  7. Sprinkle on the pine nuts, season well with salt and pepper and add a few torn basil leaves
